The Cal Vet exemption covers mandatory system-wide charges assessed by academic programs that receive State support. For state-supported programs, the specific charges covered by the Cal Vet exemption are Tuition, the Student Services Fee, and Professional Degree Supplemental Tuition. This exemption does not apply to Self-Supporting Programs. If you are unsure of the type of Tuition and Fees you are charged please contact your program or the Office of the Registrar.

In order to be found eligible for the Cal Vet College Fee Waiver you must apply annually with a County Veteran Service Office. For information and to apply please visit https://www.calvet.ca.gov/VetServices/Pages/College-Fee-Waiver.aspx.
