Requirements for Disbursement

  • Accept or decline your awards at your financial aid portal
  • Register in the quarter for which funds are to be received by filing your a study list at your student portal
  • Clear all holds
  • Check the Documents tab at your financial aid portal for any outstanding items

How Funds Are Disbursed

  • Sign-up for Electronic Fund Transfer (EFT). Failure to sign-up may result in the delays and inconveniences associated with mailing. To sign-up, you must:
    1. Register to receive Direct (Stafford) and Graduate PLUS Direct Deposit
    2. Complete Student Direct Deposit Request Form

NOTE: Direct deposit of financial aid to your bank account is available for all financial aid, including Direct (Stafford) and Graduate Plus Loans! We recommend choosing the bank account EFT option to receive funds. Choosing the debit card option will involve incurring additional costs if you intend to use the card to pay your UCSF tuition/fee balance.
