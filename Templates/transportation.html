UCSF-Lyft Student Program

UC San Francisco Transportation Services partnered with Lyft to pilot a program to provide UC San Francisco students with a Guaranteed Ride Home program. The program is designed to provide students with a commute option to reach a transit hub, other UCSF campus, or reach their home—after our shuttle network has ended for the day. The UCSF-Lyft program is designed for “after-hours” service and requires a unique Lyft code.

How it Works

Students must create a Lyft account and add their @UCSF email as either the primary or “business” email account and use the Lyft app to request a ride from Parnassus Heights or Mission Bay. If you already have a Lyft account, please add your @UCSF email to your existing account. Once registered, Lyft will send you a unique “UCSF Promo Code” and you’ll add to your account. UC San Francisco will pay the 1st $15.00 of the ride and if the ride exceeds $15.00, students pay the remainder from their personal credit card on file with the Lyft app. Lyft will send you a new unique code on a monthly basis to use for the following month. Students will have 20 rides per month to use.

Questions?

Please send any questions related to the UCSF-Lyft program to SupportMyCommute@ucsf.edu
